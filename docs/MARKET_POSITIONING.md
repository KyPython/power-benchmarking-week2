# Power Benchmarking Suite: Market Positioning & Competitive Analysis

## üéØ Product Definition

**Power Benchmarking Suite** is a **zero-to-one proprietary solution** for Apple Silicon developers and ML engineers who need to optimize energy efficiency and performance of their applications. It's the **only tool** that combines:

1. **Real-time power monitoring** (ANE, CPU, GPU, Package) during CoreML inference
2. **Statistical attribution analysis** to distinguish app power from system noise
3. **Energy Gap Framework** - a proprietary methodology for algorithm optimization
4. **Thermal throttling prediction** using physics-based models
5. **Sustainability ROI calculations** - quantifies CO2 savings from optimizations
6. **"Mechanical Sympathy" optimization** - cache-aware energy efficiency guidance

**Target Users:**
- ML engineers optimizing CoreML models for Apple Silicon
- iOS/macOS developers building battery-efficient apps
- Research teams studying Apple Silicon power characteristics
- Companies optimizing cloud costs through energy efficiency
- Sustainability-focused development teams
